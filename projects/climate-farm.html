ClimateFarm

Climate-smart agriculture automation for smallholder farmers

  • Ingests weather forecasts, soil moisture sensors, and Sentinel-2 satellite imagery
  • Predicts irrigation needs, pest outbreak risk, and optimal harvest windows
  • Sends SMS/WhatsApp alerts in local languages via Twilio
  • Connects to market prices API to suggest optimal selling times
  • Tech: Python, automation-engine, agent-core, FAISS, PostgreSQL/PostGIS
