CivicSentinel

AI-powered civic integrity monitor for detecting deepfakes and misinformation

  • Monitors Twitter/X, Facebook, Telegram for viral suspicious content
  • Uses LLaVA for computer vision to detect deepfake images/videos
  • Analyzes text for bot-like patterns and maps spread networks
  • FastAPI dashboard with real-time alerts for journalists and fact-checkers
  • Tech: Python, agent-core, LLaVA, NLTK/spaCy, NetworkX, FastAPI
